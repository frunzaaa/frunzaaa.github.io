QuickOrg2

— fast. simple. fully offline. rebuilt in Flutter.

QuickOrg2 is a complete rewrite of my personal money manager — now built with Flutter. It's designed to be simple, fast, and completely private. No internet, no cloud, no tracking. All your data stays on your phone.

Use it to organize your income and expenses, see clearly where your money goes, and stay in full control of your daily, monthly, or yearly budget. The Flutter version brings a smoother UI, better performance, and a more consistent experience across Android and in the future on iOS too.

Current features

Funds management, customizable income and expense categories, transaction history, financial calendar, detailed statistics with pie charts, backup & restore (ZIP export/import), built-in help system, light/dark themes, and native navigation with back button support. All data stays offline on your device — no internet, no tracking, no cloud.
