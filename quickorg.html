QuickOrg.

— fast. simple. fully offline.

QuickOrg is your personal mobile money manager — designed to be simple, fast, and completely private. No internet, no cloud, no tracking. All your data stays on your phone.

Use it to organize your income and expenses, see clearly where your money goes, and stay in full control of your daily, monthly, or yearly budget.

Current features

Funds

Separate accounts for your money

Create funds (cash, card, savings, etc.) and transactions automatically update each fund's balance.

Categories

Income & expenses, customizable

Categories and subcategories you can rename and organize — up to 10 each.

Transactions

Quick entry, full detail

Add a transaction in a few taps: type, category, subcategory, fund, amount, note.

Calendar

Financial calendar

Days colored by net income/expense (red → yellow → green gradient). Tap any day for detailed stats.

Stats

Pie charts with deep filters

Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Yearly views. Three-level drilldown: General → Categories → Subcategories.

Backup

Save & restore your data

Export everything to a ZIP file in /Download/QuickOrg/Backups/ and import it back anytime.

Helper

Built-in help system

"H" buttons on every panel reveal explanations and tooltips, right when you need them.

Themes

Light & Dark mode

Switch themes anytime, your choice is saved.

Navigation

Stack-based panels & Android back button

Native navigation that feels right — physical back button works as you'd expect.
