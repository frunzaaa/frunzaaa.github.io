Minutes to
Die

— control your pulse. survive the night.

Minutes to Die is a horror-survival game for Android and Windows, inspired by the oppressive atmosphere of abandoned Soviet apartment blocks and the retro style of classics like DOOM and Wolfenstein 3D.

You enter an old, dark, abandoned block. Your goal is simple: collect enough valuables and escape with your life — before the night becomes impossible to survive.

The BPM system

Instead of being killed instantly by the ghost, you have to manage your stress and heart rate. The ghost raises your BPM through proximity, chasing, direct contact, panic, and movement. If your pulse reaches a critical level, your character suffers a heart attack and the game ends.

After 3:00 AM, things get extreme: the ghost becomes permanently aggressive, your pulse can't drop anymore, and survival becomes harder by the minute.
